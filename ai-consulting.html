What It Costs: The Full Picture

Shirey CPA's implementation fee is a one-time cost. Separately, your team will need a Claude subscription from Anthropic — that's the platform everything runs on. Both costs are presented here because you deserve to know the full picture before you commit.

Anthropic Subscription (Ongoing Monthly Cost)

The AI environment Shirey CPA builds is designed around real work — document processing, financial analysis, report drafting — which drives heavier platform usage than casual AI. Monthly subscription costs typically range from $100 to $500 depending on three factors:

  • Team size and roles. More people and more complex roles mean higher costs. A 5-person team costs less than a 15-person team.
  • Usage intensity. Staff doing document-heavy work — uploading PDFs, building reports, working with financial data — need a higher-capacity plan than staff using it for quick questions.
  • Anthropic’s pricing. Anthropic sets and can change their own subscription rates at any time. Shirey CPA does not control the platform cost.

Nonprofits with 501(c)(3) status qualify for significantly reduced rates through Anthropic’s nonprofit program. Shirey CPA sizes the exact subscription configuration for your organization during discovery and provides a specific cost projection before you commit.
